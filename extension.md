### Importance of Extension

As part of the University’s land-grant mission, we are committed to education, research, and extension. I must admit that communicating with non-scientific audiences—through newsletters, television, and other media—is not always easy. However, I recognize that it is an essential way to ensure that our research extends beyond academic boundaries and reaches the broader society we serve.

Below are selected press releases and outreach materials highlighting my research.

### 2026

- Fox 4. Glades County farmer works with University of Florida researchers to make coffee a viable crop in the state [[video link]](https://www.fox4now.com/news/local-news/glades-county/glades-county-farmer-works-with-university-of-florida-researchers-to-make-coffee-a-viable-crop-in-the-state#google_vignette)
  - Collaboration in research and production with local growers in South Florida, aimed at selecting and better understanding plant performance to support the development of a farm-to-table niche in Florida.
 
- UF/HOS blog. Brewing Innovation: Developing Coffee as a Viable Florida Crop [[text link]](https://blogs.ifas.ufl.edu/hosdept/2026/01/22/coffee-genomics-florida-breeding/)
  - The importance of coffee research from the University’s perspective and the value of its partnership with the Neta Foundation.
